Funventures

We are Funventures, a group of first-year students aiming to start an activities club within XD. Our goal?Bringing everyone together through fun activities like bowling, go-karting, escape rooms, and laser tag. We want to create a place where everyone feels welcome and can have fun.

How Did We Start?
We asked our XD community what they enjoy and gathered ideas to make activities fun and affordable.

What makes us unique?

  • For Everyone: Students and teachers are welcome.
  • Lots of Variety: Active and relaxing activities.
  • Affordable and Enjoyable: Including snacks and drinks.

Our Experience?
During an escape room, we had to think hard, work together, and laugh a lot. It strengthened our bond, and we want everyone to experience that!

Mission & Vision

Mission:
Funventures brings people together for unique, unforgettable experiences filled with fun and connection.

Vision:
We aim to be the place where people relax, experience adventures, and create lifelong memories together.
